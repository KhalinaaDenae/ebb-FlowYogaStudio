Our Founding Philosophy

Experience mindfulness through yoga & meditation

At Harmony Yoga Studio our goal is to create a welcoming, inclusive space where everyone can feel comfortable and supported in their yoga practice.

We believe that yoga is not just a physical exercise, but also a pathway to inner peace, mindfulness, and self-discovery. Our classes are designed to help you connect with your body, breath, and mind, and to cultivate a greater sense of awareness, balance, and harmony in your life.

We use yoga as powerful tool for personal growth, healing, and transformation, and we are dedicated to sharing its benefits with everyone who walks through our doors.

Traditional Yoga

Vinyasa Flow

Vinyasa flow is a style of yoga where the poses are synchronized with the breath in a continuous rhythmic flow. The flow can be meditative in nature, calming the mind and nervous system, even though you’re moving. Suitable for those who’ve never tried yoga as well as those who’ve been practicing for years.

Hatha Flow

emphasizes the practice of physical postures (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama), with the goal of achieving balance between the body and mind. Hatha yoga classes are typically slower-paced and more gentle.

Ashtanga Flow

Ashtanga yoga classes are typically fast-paced and challenging, with a focus on building strength, flexibility, and endurance. Ashtanga yoga is often favored by those who are looking for a more intense workout experience.

2 / 3

Restorative Yoga

Deep Flow

Hatha inspired practice with space and time to tune inward to your body, mind and heart through movement, intention and breath. Each class incorporates poses to build strength, balance and openness. All levels

Gentle Vinyasa

Vinyasa inspired class syncing the cadence of breath with a slower flow and with less weight bearing on the wrists. Includes meditation. All levels

Gentle Flow/Easy Like Sunday Morning

Slow and gentle Hatha inspired practices with longer held poses focusing on breath. These slower practices supporting the nervous system, build resilience and strength. Most classes begin with Pranayama (breathing techniques). All levels

3 / 3

Power Yoga

Power flow/ Vinyasa

Break a sweat in these fun and challenging classes! Build strength and body awareness through active flow and static holds. Develop deep core strength for support in balance poses and inversions while exploring a deeper mind-body connection. There will be weight bearing on the wrists in these classes. Int. + Adv. Levels

Sculpt Power Yoga

incorporates strength training exercises such as lunges, squats, and push-ups into a flowing vinyasa practice. The focus is on building muscle and increasing endurance.

Core Power Yoga

Focuses on building core strength and stability. The practice includes a series of challenging poses that target the abdominal muscles, as well as the muscles of the back and hips.
